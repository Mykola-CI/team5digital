Shoplyste. Share Lists

  • Send Your List to Your Peer: Tap "Share List" Button () at the bottom left corner of the List Contents screen and you can see a usual share pop-up screen.
  • Add Club Cards: Tap the "+" button at the top bar to add a new club card. Follow the on-screen instructions.
  • Upload card: Name your card, and upload the photo of your card so your bar code or QR code is always at your fingertips when you need it.
  • Use Cards: Tap on any card in your stack to display its barcode or QR code at the checkout.
  • Remove Cards: Press the Bin button () to remove a card from your stack.
  • Edit Cards: Tap the Dotted Card icon () to edit a card. You can change the name of the card or replace the photo.
