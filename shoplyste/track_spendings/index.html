Shoplyste. Spending Tracker

  1. Shoplyste
  2. Spending Tracker
  • Add new item: Tap '+' icon at the right corner of the top bar or simply tap on empty area to add a new item. Tap '✔️' at the right corner of the top bar or at the bottom right corner of your keyboard to submit the new item.
  • Choose item from prompts: At every new entry your item (if new) will be stored in Product Library so you can see prompts next time and speed up your list creation.
  • Check off items: Check off your items to mark them as done. Simply tap on a circle at the left of an item.
  • Toggle views: Flip between Category/Aisle view and Simple List view. While in Simple List view change order of items by dragging (long-press on an item's handle at the right corner to start dragging).
  • Swipe left or right to see new options: Swipe left. Tap "+/-" buttons to set a required quantity for an item. Tap on "folder with arrows" button to assign an item with a Product Category. Swipe right to delete an item.
  • Move all items: Press the button "Move Items" at the bottom of your List content and choose shopping list you want to move your items to.
