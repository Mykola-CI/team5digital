Getting started

  • 1. Download & Launch: Get Shoplyste from the App Store, launch the app, and run through the quick onboarding screens to familiarize yourself with the features.
  • 2. Your First List: You'll land right on your default empty "Shopping List". Simply tap anywhere on the empty screen or use the '+' icon to start adding items.
  • 3. Build Up Quickly: Start typing and smart prompts will appear. Tap a suggestion to easily add the item and automatically expand your Product Library for next time!
  • 4. Shop & Check Off: While shopping, quickly check off items by tapping the circle indicator. You can also swipe an item left to adjust quantities or swipe right to delete.
  • 5. Organize & Share: Tap the spherical main menu button at the bottom right to manage multiple lists, edit custom aisles, or easily export and share a list with family or friends.
